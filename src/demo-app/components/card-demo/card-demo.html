Cards

The MDC Card component is a sheet of material that serves as an entry point to more detailed information.
Component
mdc-card
Properties Description
stroked: boolean Removes the shadow and displays a hairline stroke instead
Directive
mdc-card-primary-action The main tappable area of the card. Typically contains most (or all) card content except mdc-card-actions. Only applicable to cards that have a primary action that the main surface should trigger.
Component
mdc-card-media
Properties Description
square: boolean Automatically scales the media area's height to equal its width.
rectangle: boolean Automatically scales the media area's height according to its width, maintaining a 16:9 aspect ratio.
Directive
mdc-card-media-content An absolutely-positioned box the same size as the media area, for displaying a title or icon on top of the background-image
Component
mdc-card-actions Row containing action buttons and/or icons
Properties Description
fullBleed: boolean Removes the action area's padding and causes its only child (an mdc-card__action element) to consume 100% of the action area's width.
icons: boolean A group of supplemental action icons, displayed on the right side of the card (in LTR), adjacent to mdc-card-action buttons
buttons: boolean A group of action buttons, displayed on the left side of the card (in LTR), adjacent to mdc-card-action icons
Directive
mdc-card-action-icons A group of supplemental action icons, displayed on the right side of the card (in LTR), adjacent to mdc-card-action buttons
Directive
mdc-card-action-buttons A group of action buttons, displayed on the left side of the card (in LTR), adjacent to mdc-card-action icons
Directive
mdcCardAction An individual action button or icon. Provide either `icon` or `button`. (e.g.: mdcCardAction="icon" or mdcCardAction="button")
